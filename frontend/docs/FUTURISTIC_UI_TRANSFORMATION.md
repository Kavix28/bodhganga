- **Primary Font:** Space Grotesk (headings) - Bold, futuristic
- **Secondary Font:** Plus Jakarta Sans (body) - Modern, readable  
- **H1:** 5xl-7xl (80-96px) - Maximum impact
- **H2:** 4xl-6xl (56-72px) - Strong hierarchy
- **Weight:** 700-900 - Extra bold for confidence


- Layered depth with shadows

### Neon Glow Effects

- Animated gradient shifts

## 🎓 EDUCATIONAL PSYCHOLOGY PRINCIPLES

### 1. **Visual Hierarchy**
- Most important info (CTAs) are largest & boldest
- Clear reading flow: headline → subheadline → CTA

- **Purple/Indigo** - Creativity, wisdom, learning

### 4. **Motivation Triggers**
